Quadro Cloud is your dedicated client portal — manage your service contracts, invoices, and support requests all from one place.

KEY FEATURES

Dashboard
Get an instant overview of your next payment due date, overdue invoices, and pending additional fees the moment you open the app. A countdown card shows exactly how many days remain before your next bill is due, color-coded so urgent items always stand out.

Contracts
View all your active and past service contracts in one list. See renewal dates, contract status, and billing cycle at a glance. The app alerts you when a contract is expiring within 7 days so you're never caught off guard.

Invoices
Browse your full invoice history and see exactly what you owe. Pay outstanding balances securely via the integrated payment gateway — directly inside the app without switching to a browser.

Payment History
Access a complete, chronological record of every transaction. Confirm past payments and reference amounts whenever you need them.

Support Tickets
Submit a support request and track its progress without picking up the phone or opening your email. See ticket status, category, and priority in real time.

News & Services
Browse the latest company announcements and explore the full catalog of available services and packages. Stay informed about new offerings and promotions.

Push Notifications
Receive instant alerts for upcoming payment deadlines, new invoices, and support ticket replies — so nothing slips through the cracks.

Arabic & English
Fully supports both Arabic (right-to-left) and English. Switch at any time in Settings to work in your preferred language.

Dark Mode
A carefully designed dark theme ensures comfortable reading day or night.
